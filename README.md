# Deliveroid-2.0
Second iteration of the Deliveroid delivery robot

## Motor Wiring
#### Left Motors
- Enable Pin: 5
- Back Left Wheel: 1
- Front Left Wheel: 2

#### Right Motors
- Enable Pin: 6
- Back Right Wheel: 3
- Front Left Wheel: 4

## NRF24L01 Wiring (on board antenna)(arduino)
- CE: 9
- SCK: 13
- MISO: 12
- CSN: 10
- MOSI: 8
- IRQ: NULL

## NRF24L01 Wiring (on board antenna)(raspberry pi)
- CE: GPIO17
- SCK: SCLK
- MISO: MISO
- CSN: CE0
- MOSI: MOSI
- IRQ: NULL
